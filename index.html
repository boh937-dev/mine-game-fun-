Tower Defense

            
Use your unlocked characters as towers. Click a character in the bottom roster bar, then click the arena to place them.

            
            

              
Each Common: place up to 6

              
Each Rare: place up to 4

              
Each Epic: place up to 3

              
Each Mythic: place up to 2

            

            

              
            

          


          

            
How It Works

            
Waves get stronger over time. Towers auto-throw dodgeballs and auto-use their ability every 5 seconds. You earn more coins the longer your base survives.

            

              
Place towers above or below the path.

              
Towers can overlap if you want to stack them.

              
Enemies that reach the right side damage your base.

              
Every 5th wave spawns a boss and later bosses are stronger.

              
Press ESC to leave and keep the coins you earned in the run.

              
Reset Match restarts the current tower defense run.
